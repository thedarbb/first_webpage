_Story

Alright, so my name is Dustin Sappier. People call me Darb. I'm just a small town boy, living in a lonely world. I took the midnight train going anywhere.

All jokes aside - While Darb's Kitchen is completely and totally fictitious.. I do enjoy cooking. So I decided to share a bit of my favorite recipes and what not. I never actually knew how to cook until I lived on my own. I didn't own a microwave so the easy stuff was off the table. From there I would take what I could find in my cupboards, refridgerator, and freezer. With the gathered ingredients I would kind of just come up with something from nothing. It was like magic!

And thus a legend was born!
